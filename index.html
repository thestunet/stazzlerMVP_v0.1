How it Work?

Everyone loves Simplicity, so do we! Stazzlers creates a simplified solution for both speakers and Organizers to connect and share their knowledge to the world!

  • Speaker's Journey
  • Organizer's Journey
Step 1

Register with Stazzler as a Speaker

As a speaker, the first thing you need to do is register with us and be ready to get Stazzled!

Step 2

List your profile.

Add some glory to your profile by updating your skills, publishing interesting articles and upload your sample videos.

Step 3

Suit up! its time to fill your calendar with events

We will now route the enquiries and proposals from the event organisers for you to check and accept. Speakers can also browse our events for more oppurtunities

Step 1

Register with Stazzler as an Organizer

As an organizer, the first thing you need to do is register in Stazzler. Trust us, you will be Stazzled!

Step 2

List your events

Showcase your upcoming events with speaker requirement on topic and budget willing to spend, where speakers can send their willingness to speak in your event.

Step 3

Choose the best suited speaker as per your budget, topic, location, etc.

Here starts your hunt from our unique and talented list of speakers. Find the one from our large database of talented speakers

Do you have Questions?

Please feel free to read the below.

What are the benefits of using Stazzler?

For Event Organisers - Stazzler provides a solution in finding the best speaker based on your requirement

For Speakers - Stazzler helps in finding the oppurtunities to showcase their talents and helps to spread the knowledge

Do you charge any commission or hidden charges?

Stazzler is completely free for event organisers. Speakers can create their own profile for free, however to enjoy additional benefits and continue receiving leads - please subscribe our package deal

Dont you worry, Speakers get to enjoy the complimentary benefit of one month free subscription on each successfull referal

Is this platform only for established speakers?

Stazzler also welcomes the emerging speakers who are looking for oppurtunity to showcase their talent and aspiring to becoming the best.

Does Stazzlers have speakers who speak for cause or charity?

There are various speakers available for free, who just like to showcase their skills to the world and some who would like to work for charity. Stazzler helps them to get FOUND
